Public Health Solution

An AI-powered platform that monitors population health data, flags emerging trends, and supports epidemiological decision-making — helping health authorities act before crises escalate.

Public health systems generate enormous volumes of data every day — from hospital admissions and disease reports to environmental sensors and social media signals. The challenge is not a lack of data, but the ability to connect it, interpret it, and act on it in time.

The Sojaa.ai Public Health Solution is an AI-powered surveillance and decision-support platform designed for health ministries, regional authorities, and public health agencies. It aggregates data from multiple sources in real time, identifies emerging patterns, and surfaces actionable insights — so decision-makers can respond to outbreaks and health crises before they become unmanageable.

The platform includes anomaly detection for unusual case clusters, risk stratification by geography and demographics, automated reporting for health authorities, and integration with existing epidemiological workflows.

Key capabilities
Real-time disease surveillance
Outbreak early warning system
Geospatial risk mapping
Automated epidemiological reports
Integration with national health records
Multilingual dashboard
